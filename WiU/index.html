Project Name: WorldSurf

World Surf

Project WorldSurf is about challenging society to get involved in preserving and supporting surfing culture. True surfer bums are a dying breed, and have been brought to the edge of extinction. The reason for their steep decline is government bureaucrats and their clean surf agenda. With the help of concerned citizens, WorldSurf has raised over 2 million U.S. dollars in order to preserve the surf culture. Please think about joining WorldSurf, by donating today toward this important part of America.
